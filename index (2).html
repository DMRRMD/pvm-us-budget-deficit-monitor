How to read the numbers

Short plain-English notes designed for readers who do not follow Treasury reporting every month.

Latest monthly deficit

The newest monthly gap between what the federal government spent and what it collected.

FYTD deficit

The cumulative deficit for the current fiscal year so far, not the calendar year.

Rolling 12M deficit

The sum of the last 12 monthly readings, which smooths out one-off monthly swings.

Total public debt

The total outstanding federal debt reported by Treasury's daily Debt to the Penny series.
